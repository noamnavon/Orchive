Musical Traits Classification

Have you ever been engulfed in a melody, yet find yourself at a loss to define its genre? Do you search for a certain musical ambiance, only to find the style too broad or inconsistent? The issue lies in the nature of genres: they're more of a chronological chart of artistic influences than a precise reflection of the music's inherent qualities.

Enter the Musical Traits Classification (MTC). This system transcends traditional boundaries by focusing on the core attributes of music. It's a tool that distills the essence of a piece into a five-letter code, allowing for more nuanced recommendations and a deeper understanding of musical identities.

With MTC, embark on a journey of musical discovery, where vibes are mapped, and every note's purpose is crystal clear.

Energy

Vigorous - Lethargic

This trait is about the energy of the music.

High energy results in higher heart beat rate, more activity in the brain, excitement and ultimately thrill.
Vigorous music hallmarks:
~ Fast
~ Loud
~ Dense

Low energy tend to have more chill and breathy vibes, leading to apathy and lower brain activity.
Lethargic music hallmarks:
~ Slow
~ Quiet
~ Sparse

Structure

Complex - Simple

This trait is about the structure of the music.

Complex music is changing, deep and decorated, often requires several listenings to fully comprehend it.
Complex music hallmarks:
~ Detailed
~ Turbulent
~ Dimensional

Simple music is neat, memorable, steady, and often catchy, creating hooks and earworms.
Simple music hallmarks:
~ Minimalist
~ Stable
~ Flat

Tone

Bright - Dark

This trait is about the tone of the music.

Bright tone describes good, joy, hope, peace and positive emotions, so it can lift the mood when needed.
Bright music hallmarks:
~ Optimistic
~ Soothe
~ Leviate

Dark tone describes evil, sorrow, despair, struggle and negative emotions, mostly serious and heavy.
Dark music hallmarks:
~ Pessimistic
~ Harsh
~ Gravitate

Heritage

Traditional - Progressive

This trait is about the heritage of the music.

If the genre is easily identified, the progression is familiar, or you can predict the music, it is traditional.
Traditional music hallmarks:
~ Generic
~ Conformist
~ Anchored

If you can't put your finger on it, and the music evolves in direction you wouldn't expect, it is progressive.
Progressive music hallmarks:
~ Eclectic
~ Rebel
~ Wondering

Mind

Emotion - Intellect

This trait is about the mind of the music.

Emotional music is subjective, spout from the heart and touch deeply, engages in feeling the man behind.
Emotional music hallmarks:
~ Expression
~ Relation
~ Purpose

Intellectual music is objective, structured by the mind and stimulate the brain, invites to decipher the piece.
Intellectual music hallmarks:
~ Technique
~ Analysis
~ Abstraction
