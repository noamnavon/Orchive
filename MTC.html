Musical Traits Classification

Have you ever been engulfed in a melody, yet find yourself at a loss to define its genre? Do you search for a certain musical ambiance, only to find the style too broad or inconsistent? The issue lies in the nature of genres: they're more of a chronological chart of artistic influences than a precise reflection of the music's inherent qualities.

Enter the Musical Traits Classification (MTC). This system transcends traditional boundaries by focusing on the core attributes of music. It's a tool that distills the essence of a piece into a five-letter code, allowing for more nuanced recommendations and a deeper understanding of musical identities.

With MTC, embark on a journey of musical discovery, where vibes are mapped, and every note's purpose is crystal clear.

Energy

Vigorous - Lethargic

This trait is about the energy of the music.

High energy results in higher heart beat rate, and excitement
Vigorous music is:
~ Fast
~ Loud
~ Dense

Low energy tend to have more chill and breathy vibes.
Lethargic music is:
~ Slow
~ Quiet
~ Sparse

Complication

Intricate - Minimalist

This trait is about the complication of the music.

Intricate music is changing, deep and decorated, often requires several listenings to fully comprehend it.
Intricate music is:
~ Detailed
~ Turbulent
~ Dimensional

Minimalist music is simple, memorable, steady, and often catchy, creating hooks and earworms.
Minimalist music is:
~ Neat
~ Stable
~ Flat

Tone

Bright - Dark

This trait is about the tone of the music.

Bright tone describes good, joy, hope, peace and positive emotions.
Bright music has:
~ Consonants
~ Soothe
~ Levity

Dark tone describes evil, sorrow, despair, struggle and negative emotions.
Minimalist music is:
~ Dissonants
~ Grit
~ Gravity

Heritage

Traditional - Progressive

This trait is about the heritage of the music.

If the genre is easily identified, the progression is familiar, or you can predict the music, it is Traditional.
Traditional music is:
~ Generic
~ Conformist
~ Anchored

If you can't put your finger on it, and the music evolves in direction you wouldn't expect, it is Progressive.
Progressive music is:
~ Eclectic
~ Rebel
~ Wondering

Spirit

Soulful - Analytical

This trait is about the spirit of the music.

Soulful music is subjective, spout from the heart and touch deeply.
The bold features are:
~ Expression
~ Emotion
~ Intent

Analytical music is objective, structured by the mind and stimulate the brain.
The bold features are:
~ Technique
~ Intellect
~ Abstraction
